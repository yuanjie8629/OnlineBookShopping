A Better Way to Buy Books Online

Established in January 2020, OBS Pte Ltd has become a household name in Singapore and has now grown to more than 45 stores nationwide. Online Book Shopping (OBS) began as an idea to help support the bookstores at a time when more and more people were buying their books online. OBS Pte Ltd is 100% managed by Singaporeans and is strongly positioned as a trilingual bookstore catering for everyone from all walks of life.
Our Mission
To serve our people, to grow with our society and to mutually benefit from the world in the age of ecommerce.
Our Vision
To be a leader and dynamic bookstore company.
To provide value-for-money products and quality services to our customers.
To excel in the online book shopping market and maintain the presence of the company in local communities.
